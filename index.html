About the Generator

On every bingo card you will find the following squares:

Race Starts

This square appears at the center of every bingo card. It has 23 race starts that must be crossed off before you can cross off the bingo square. It's upto you wether you choose to count race restarts as race starts.

Safety Car

This square appears in every bingo card, but in a random location. The intention is for the numbers to be crossed off when the safety car goes on track until you reach 10 safety cars, then cross off the square. The Virtual Safety Car does not count towards this square.

Virtual Safety Car

This square appears in every bingo card, but in a random location. The intention is for the numbers to be crossed off when the virtual safety car takes effect on track until you reach 8 virtual safety cars, then cross off the square. The regular Safety Car does not count towards this square.

Red Flag

This square appears in every bingo card, but in a random location. The intention is for the numbers to be crossed off when the red flag is shown on track until you reach 4 red flags, then cross off the square.

On every bingo card you will find a random 21 of the following squares:

Max has won the WDC

When Max (inevitably) wins the 2023 World Drivers' Championship.

First time winner

When the winner of a race did not have a race win in Formula 1 before that race. It is upto you if you count sprint race wins for this square.

No Red Bull, Mercedes, or Ferrari appears on the podium

When no Red Bull, Mercedes, or Ferrari appears on the podium. Everyone loves a good underdog story! Especially when it's everyone on the podium.

Lando Norris wins a race

When Lando Norris gets his first win in Formula 1. It's only a matter of time, right guys? Right? It's upto you if a sprint race win counts for this square.

First lap crash

When there is a crash during the first lap of a race. Two are required for this square.

Lewis has won the WDC

When Lewis wins the 2023 World Drivers' Championship. This ones a bit of a long shot this year, but it's here for the die hard Lewis fans.

Wet Race

When rain falls during a race. Two are required for this square. It's upto you if rain during a sprint race counts for this square.

Wet Qualifying

When rain falls during qualifying. Two are required for this square.

First time pole

When P1 during a qualifying session is the first time a driver has ever been on pole. It's upto you if you count a first pole from a sprint race for this square.
